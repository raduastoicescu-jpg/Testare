About this space

Charming and homey condo in Floreasca — one of Bucharest's most vibrant neighbourhoods, with fine restaurants, bars, parks and the beautiful Lake Floreasca just steps away. Despite its central placement, the surroundings remain serene and inviting.

Ideal for couples, solo explorers, work trips and families. The apartment comes fully furnished and equipped for short and long stays. Unwind on the spacious balcony after a day of culinary adventures and sightseeing — you have everything you need and more.

Situated 100m from parks and lakes on the 4th floor of a recently renovated building. Your host Maria is always a message away — guests consistently highlight her speed and warmth.
