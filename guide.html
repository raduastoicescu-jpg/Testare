Gas Oven & Hob
Gas appliance — the apartment has a smoke & gas alarm for your safety

Gas hob (stovetop)

1

Find the knob for the ring you want. Each knob is labelled GAZ. Push the knob in gently and turn it to the right.

2

While holding the knob pushed in, press the separate igniter button (the small round button — see photos below). You'll hear a click and the flame will light.

3

Keep the knob pressed for a few extra seconds after the flame appears so the safety thermostat can engage. Then release slowly.

4

To turn off: rotate the knob back to the closed (vertical/off) position.

Gas oven

5

Open the oven door and lift the small trap door at the base to access the burner. Find the knob labelled CUPTOR, push it in gently and turn it to the right.

6

While holding the knob pressed in, hold a match or lighter (available in the apartment) to the burner — the built-in igniter does not work for the oven. Once lit, keep the knob pressed for a few extra seconds, then release slowly.

7

To turn off: rotate the CUPTOR knob back to the closed (vertical/off) position.

If you see No Signal: press SOURCE / INPUT on the Samsung remote → select HDMI 1 or HDMI 2 (the one connected to the UPC receiver). Then use only the UPC remote.

📱 Use Smart TV (Netflix / YouTube / apps)

1

Press SOURCE / INPUT on the Samsung remote.

2

Select TV / Smart Hub / Home (NOT HDMI).

3

Navigate apps with the Samsung remote only.

🎮 Simple rule

📦 UPC remote = TV channels

📺 Samsung remote = Apps / Smart TV

🔁 SOURCE button = switch between them

⚠️ No signal? → Press SOURCE and change HDMI input

⚠️ Can't change channels? → You're in Smart TV mode — switch to HDMI

⚠️ Apps not working? → Switch back to Smart Hub / Home

💡 Lost? → Press the 🏠 Home button on the Samsung remote to return to Smart TV
